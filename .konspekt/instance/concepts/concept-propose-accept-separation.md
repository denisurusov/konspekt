# Concept: Propose–accept separation

Proposing a change and accepting it are distinct acts: the maintainer only ever
proposes, acceptance is exclusively human. The separation is what keeps an
LLM-maintained instance auditable, and is the through-line of reconciliation,
review, and ingestion.
