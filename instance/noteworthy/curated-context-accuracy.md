# Noteworthy (assumption): Curated context increases LLM accuracy

Feeding human-approved / used / preferred context to an LLM will *possibly*
increase the accuracy and relevance of its output. Plausible via higher
signal-to-noise — but curation can also drop coverage or bias the model toward
the human's framing, improving relevance at a possible cost to coverage.
Unvalidated; needs testing. Surfaces in the open-assumptions view.
